About Turbo Car

Turbo Car is a low-poly racing game with loops, jumps, and high speeds, where every millisecond counts. The game is heavily inspired by TrackMania, where you race against the clock to improve your time on different tracks.

The game includes a level editor where you can create tracks, which you can then export and share with others. Whether you have five minutes or an hour to play, Turbo Car offers an engaging experience that keeps you coming back for "just one more run" as you try to beat your previous best time.

Game instructions: WASD or the arrow keys to drive. R or enter to restart the track.
